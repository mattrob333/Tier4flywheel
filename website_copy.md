## 4. Flywheel Stages Details (StageCards)

**Stage 01: Programmatic SEO & AI Web Presence**
- **Short Detail:** 500–2,000+ hyper-local pages. LLM.txt optimized. $0/lead organic traffic. (70% reduction in cost-per-lead)
- **Full Detail:** 500–2,000+ hyper-local pages auto-generated on your existing domain. LLM.txt optimization ensures your business is cited by AI search engines — not just indexed by Google. Shifts your lead mix from 50%+ paid to 30% organic — permanently.

**Stage 02: AI Voice & Chat Agents**
- **Short Detail:** 24/7 voice + chat. 95%+ answer rate. Zero missed revenue. ($250–$350 saved per recovered call)
- **Full Detail:** Every call answered. Every chat engaged. Every after-hours lead captured. Our AI agents speak naturally, qualify leads, book appointments, and sync directly to your existing field service platform — 24/7/365. No missed revenue. No voicemail. No excuses.

**Stage 03: Lead Scoring & Follow-Up**
- **Short Detail:** Under 2-minute response. Intelligent qualification & routing. (400% improvement in lead qualification)
- **Full Detail:** A 10-minute delay drops qualification by 400%. Our system responds in under 2 minutes. Every lead scored, prioritized, and routed to the right technician — automatically. The 'estimate graveyard' (60–70% of quotes that never close) gets automated follow-up sequences that recover revenue you already paid to acquire.

**Stage 04: Intelligent Dispatch**
- **Short Detail:** AI-optimized routing. Best tech to best job. Every time. (10% drive time reduction, +12% ticket size)
- **Full Detail:** AI dispatch reads every job, every technician's skill profile, every route — and makes the optimal match instantly. Integrates with your existing dispatch platform. 10% reduction in drive time. 8–12% increase in utilization and average ticket. Adds 'virtual trucks' to your fleet without a single hire.

**Stage 05: Reputation & Reviews**
- **Short Detail:** Automated requests. Sentiment routing. SEO keyword seeding. (Feeds back into Stage 1 → compounds)
- **Full Detail:** Every completed job triggers an automated review request. Happy customers are routed to Google. Unhappy customers are intercepted for resolution before they go public. AI writes personalized responses seeded with local SEO keywords. More reviews → higher rankings → more organic leads → the flywheel accelerates.
